Kangband

Kangband is an exercise in keeping my programming skills intact as well as a fun diversion. It is also a way to give something back to the net for all the things I have received. Kangband is a variant of Angband 2.8.2. It takes the standard game and adds features while maintaining the original flavor. Additions include an expanded town, terrains, new spells, and quests.
