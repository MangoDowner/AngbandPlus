Changelog v0.1.6 (Engine)

2003-05-08  Stig E Sandø <stig@users.sourceforge.net>
	* Moved to ogg-format for sound-effects
	* Added external (ie not in Lisp) sound-daemon
	* Made simple strategy-system for monsters, including a primitive
	attacker and a peaceful mover
	* Improved checking of variant-data
	* Revamped settings-system to use simple hashtable-inheritance
	instead of classes.  Easier to maintain and change for users.
	* Upgraded theme-code to do more sophisticated calculations of
	positioning based on other windows' sizes.
	* Added bigscreen-support to curses/gcu
	* Added system to show visual states graphically in SDL-version
	* Split engine and variants into separate Common Lisp packages,
	and added necessary exports.
	* Added hunger
	* Fixed several TTF-issues and overall font-code
	* Made sure that all parts of lbui.so/lbui.dll use common prefixes
	to avoid namespace collisions.
	* Made select-item reduce flashing windows
	* Fixed targeting bugs
	* Many other small improvements, bug-fixes and issues.
