#####R === T.o.M.E. Magic - Tribal Realm ===

The Tribal Realm is very shamanistic in nature, drawing it's power from
the awesome forces of nature. It endows the caster with the ability to
become as one with the natural world, but these magics come at a price - 
this realm can be dangerous!

It contains some powerful attack spells, some self-altering spells, and
a few healing spells.

#####GPermitted Classes
#####BThe Tribal Realm is only permitted to:
Beastmasters as a primary realm
Monks as a primary realm
Rangers as a primary realm

#####GTribal Spellbooks
The are four books available for the Tribal realm, two of which can be
easily found at bookstores back in town. The other two are very rare, and may
only be found by searching the depths of the dungeon.
