DUNADAN
---------

Dunadans are a race of hardy men from the west. They are
stronger than the regular human in every ways, and they
are better than humans at anything they do. However, since
they already know a little about everyting, learning is
harder and they tend to learn skills more slowly than the
average race.

ADVANTAGES: +20% to all stats

DISADVANTAGES: Must gain more experience to level up.
