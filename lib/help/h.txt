HALF-TROLL
------------

Half-Trolls are the result of human and troll breeding.
Similar to the Half-Orcs in many ways, the Half-Trolls are
even uglier and dumber than the Half-Orcs, but they are
tougher and have inherited the very useful regenerative powers
of their troll parents.

ADVANTAGES: +33% to Strength
            +33% to Constitution
	    Innate regeneration

DISADVANTAGES: -50% to Intelligence
	       -33% to Wisdom
	       -33% to Charisma
