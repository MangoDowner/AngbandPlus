#####R=== Ents ===

#####GDescription
The Ents are a powerful race dating from the beginning of the world, eldest
of all animals or plants who inhabit Arda. Spirits of the land, they were 
summoned to guard the forests of Middle-earth. Being much like trees they 
are very slow but strong, and very susceptible to fire. As the Shepherds 
of the Trees, they have the innate ability to cause trees to rise about 
them for protection.
