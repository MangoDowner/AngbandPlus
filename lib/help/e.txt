GNOME
-------

Gnomes looks like dwarves, but are smaller and weaker than
the dwarves. However, they are smart and stealthy, and
generally make very good mages, but their lack of physical
strength and constitution makes them somewhat inept to melee
combat.

ADVANTAGES: +25% to Intelligence
            +20% to Stealth skill

DISADVANTAGES: -33% to Strength
               -33% to Constitution
