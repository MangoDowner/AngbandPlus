BARBARIAN
-----------

Barbarians are the hardy men from the north. Barbarians
are wild, strong and tough. Their combat skills are
amazing, and they are able to fight with any melee
weapons, and their throwing skill is also very good.
As great, ferocious warriors, they are fearless, but
their magic skill leave much to be desired, as they
completely shun magic.

ADVANTAGES: +20% to Strength
            +20% to Constitution
            +20% to Swords, Hafted, Polearms, Axes and Throwing
            skills.
            Innate resistance to fear

DISADVANTAGES: -25% to Intelligence
               -25% to Wisdom
	       -50% to Spellcraft and Rod skills.
