#####R === PernAngband Magic - Chaos Realm ===

The minor realm of Choas is the domain of Chaos Warriors and Daemonologists,
and deals with magics of destruction, calling on the power that exists in
the underlying chaos of the universe.

The chaos realm contains many powerful attack spells, but not many utility
and no healing type spells.

#####GPermitted Classes
#####BThe Realm of Chaos is only permitted to:
Chaos Warriors as a primary realm
Daemonologists as a secondary realm
Sorcerors (all)

#####GChaos Spellbooks
The are four spellbooks available for the Chaos realm, two of which can be
easily found at bookstores back in town. The other two are very rare, and 
may only be found by searching the depths of the dungeon.
