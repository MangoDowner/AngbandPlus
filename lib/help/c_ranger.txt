#####R=== Rangers ===

#####GDescription
Rangers are warrior/mages, devoted to nature. They are good fighters, 
and quite effective with bows. The ranger learns spells much more slowly
than a mage, but is capable of learning their choice of spells in their 
realm. Rangers have good stealth, perception, searching, saving throws,
and are good with magical devices. 

#####GPrimary Stats
Intelligence (spellcasting stat)
Dexterity.

#####GMagic Usage
At one with the wilderness,  rangers may choose among the realms of 
*****m_illus.txt*0[Illusion] or *****m_tribal.txt*0[Tribal] magic. They have no limitations as to which books 
they are allowed to use, but can learn only up to 32 spells no matter 
which realm they choose.

#####GStarting Equipment
A ranger begins the game with:
    L1 spellbook of tribal magic
    L1 spellbook of illusion magic
    A broadsword
If they are *very* lucky they may also begin with:
    a short bow
