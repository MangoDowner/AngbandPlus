#####R === Conglomoband Magic - Prayer Realm ===

The Prayer realm is there for those who wish to play with the traditional
Vanilla (standard) Angband priest spells.

It contains a few attack spells, some utility spells, some combat-
enhancing spells, and many healing spells.

#####GPermitted Classes
#####BThe Realm of Prayer is only permitted to:
Priests as a primary realm

#####GPrayer Spellbooks
The are nine spellbooks available for the Prayer realm, four of which can be
easily found at bookstores back in town. The other five are very rare, and may
only be found by searching the depths of the dungeon.
