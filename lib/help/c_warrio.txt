#####R=== Warriors ===

#####GDescription
A Warrior is a hack-and-slash character, who solves most of his problems
by cutting them to pieces, but will occasionally fall back on the help 
of a magical device. Their prime stats are Strength and Constitution, 
and a good Dexterity can really help at times. A Warrior will be good 
at Fighting and Throwing/Bows, but bad at most other skills.
A Warrior learns no magic.

#####GPrimary Stats
Strength
Constitution

#####GMagic Usage
Warriors cast no spells. They hate magic. In fact, they even gain 
experience for destroying high level spellbooks.

#####GStarting Equipment
A warrior begins the game with:
    A ring of fear resistance
    A broadsword
    Chain Mail
If they are *very* lucky they may also begin with:
    a scroll of *enchant weapon*
