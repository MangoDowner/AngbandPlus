CELESTIAL
-----------

The Celestials or Astarians are spiritual scholars and artisans. 
They appear as winged humans or elves of surpassing beauty and grace. 
This does not prevent them from flying into battle with their deadly 
spears and polearms and unleashing the powerful magics they master.


ADVANTAGES: +33% to Dex/Wis/Cha
	    +20% to Polearms and Spellcraft
            Innate flying
            Innate resistance to Light
	    Innate resistance to experience draining
            Sustained Wisdom, Dexterity, Charisma
	    Light Ball ability

DISADVANTAGES: -10% to Strength
	       -10% to Constitution
	       Need more experience to level up
