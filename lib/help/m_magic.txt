#####R === Conglomoband Magic - Magic Realm ===

The Magic realm is there for those who wish to play with the traditional
Vanilla (standard) Angband mage spells.

It contains some attack spells, some utility spells, some combat-
enhancing spells, and a few minor healing spells.

#####GPermitted Classes
#####BThe Realm of Magic is only permitted to:
Mages as a primary realm

#####GMagic Spellbooks
The are nine spellbooks available for the Magic realm, four of which can be
easily found at bookstores back in town. The other five are very rare, and may
only be found by searching the depths of the dungeon.
