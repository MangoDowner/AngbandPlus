#####R === PernAngband Magic - Shadow Realm ===

The Realm of Shadow draws it's power from those things that lie hidden
in the dark. It is magically the opposite of the Magery realm.

It contains many attack spells, some utility spells, some combat-
enhancing spells, and a few healing spells.

#####GPermitted Classes
#####BThe Realm of Shadow is only permitted to:
High Mages as a primary realm
Mages as a primary realm
Rogues as a primary realm
Sorcerors (all)

#####GShadow Spellbooks
The are nine spellbooks available for the Shadow realm, four of which can be
easily found at bookstores back in town. The other five are very rare, and may
only be found by searching the depths of the dungeon.
