HALF-ORC
----------

Half-Orcs are the result of human and orc breeding. Ugly,
unwise and somewhat stupid, Half-Orcs makes up for that
with their very high strength and good constitution. Like
the dwarves, they live in darkness, and thus are resistant
to blindness and can see in the dark.

ADVANTAGES: +33% to Strength
            +25% to Constitution
	    Innate resistance to blindness
	    Innate resistance to darkness attacks
	    Innate dark vision

DISADVANTAGES: -33% to Intelligence
	       -25% to Wisdom
	       -25% to Charisma
