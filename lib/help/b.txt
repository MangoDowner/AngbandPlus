HALF-ELF
----------

Half-Elves are a cross between a human and an elf. They share 
the characterictics of the two races to a lesser extent. 
Half-Elves makes better ranged attackers than melee fighters, but 
they are far from being weak at melee combat.

ADVANTAGES: +10% to Dexterity
            +5% to Agility skill

DISADVANTAGES: -10% to Strength
               -5% to Constitution
