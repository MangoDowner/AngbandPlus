
			  Advanced Theology
			  -----------------

Herein is presented a carefully compiled list of benefits for high-level
disciples of the 16 elemental Gods controlling the pits of Angband:
Note that the entry-level and medium-level benefits still apply.

Chernobog, the God of Night -- Telepathy, an unholy aura of light, and 
                               aggravation.
Belobog, the God of Day     -- N/A
Melkor, the God of Shadow   -- Resistance to cold, regeneration.
Svarog, the God of Fire     -- Immunity to fire.
Chthon, the God of Blood    -- A more dextrous body.
Mokosh, the God of Nature   -- N/A
Naunet, the God of Water    -- Slow digestion.
Voltumna, the God of Stone  -- Resistance to shards.
Shaitan, the God of Evil    -- Resistance to disenchantment and chaos, as well
                               as permanent berserk.
Amon-Ras, the God of Good   -- A wiser mind.
Apep, the God of Chaos      -- Resistance to life drain.
Radogast, the God of Sun    -- Regeneration.
Herovit, the God of Rage    -- Free action, resistance to blindness and 
                               confusion.
Veles, the God of Beasts    -- A stronger and more dextrous body.
Stribog, the God of Winds   -- Permanent speed.
Chenti, the God of Spheres  -- Resistance to blindness, confusion, and fear.
