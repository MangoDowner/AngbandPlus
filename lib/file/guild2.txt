Want magical powers beyond your imagination?  Join the mage guild now, and
your dreams will become reality.  Magii can use magic to defeat, deceive,
confuse and escape from their enemies.  Magic devices will almost activate
themselves, and you will be able to resist the effects of spells cast
against you.  Becoming a mage will increase your intelligence, wisdom,
dexterity and charisma, and provide you with many magical spells.

Becoming a mage is easy!  Join up now, free of charge, and collect your
free equipment to get you started.  You will be provided with the first
book of magical spells, a dagger, an identification scroll and a special
bonus item as our way of saying welcome.
