CONDITION TYPES FOR ANSWERS:

0. None
1. Event condition. Parameter 1 is event number, parameter 2 is event's value.
2. Item condition. Parameter 1 is item's tval, parameter 2 is items's sval.
3. Monster slain condition. Parameter 1 is monster's index. Parameter 2 is death count.
4. Alignment condition. Parameter  1 is the value. If positive, alignment must 
   be at least this value. If negative, the alignment must be at most this value.
5. Charisma condition. Parameter 1 is the amount. To tell that answer, your 
   Charisma must be equal or higher than that amount.
6. Race condition. Parameter 1 is the race id. You must be that race for this answer
   to appear.
7. Gender condition. Parameter 1 is the gender id. You must be that gender for this
   answer to appear.

EFFECTS OF ANSWERS:

Param 4 is always an event, and param 5 is the event's value. When you select this
answer, the events will be set to the specified values.

0. End dialog.
1. Go to next dialog block. Param 1 is dialog's number.
2. End dialog and change town. Param 1 is town's number, Param 2 is x position, Param 3 is y position.
3. Give item, go next dialog block. Param 1 is item's number. Param 2 is item's magic level(0: Normal, 1: Good, 2: Great, 3: Special 4: Artifact, param 1 becomes the artifact's number.), or gold amount. Param 3 is next dialog.
4. Go to random dialog block. Param 1 is minimum dialog number, Param 2 is max number.
5. Affect alignment. Param 1 is the amount. Param 2 is the next dialog.
6. Take item, go next dialog block. Param 1 is the item's number. Param 2 is the next dialog.
7. Alter town, go to next dialog block. Param 1 is which town, param 2 is the new town template to use. Param 3 is next dialog.
8. Pay gold. Param 1 is the amount.  Param 2 is dialog if enough money. Param 3 is dialog if not enough money.
9. Remove all monsters of a given race. Param 1 is the race index. Param 2 is next dialog.
10. Make all monsters of a given race friendly or hostile. Param 1 is the race index. Set param 2 to 0 for hostile, or to 1 for friendly. Param 3 is next dialog.
11. Run a script. It calls "dialog_script", and param 1 is script's id. Param 2 is next dialog.
