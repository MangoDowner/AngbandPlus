Preview of Langband v0.1.1

After the last release of Langband some radical decisions have been made and implemented. The upcoming release will be heavily affected by full revamp of UI. First the more dramatic:

  • main-win has been dropped and will not work
  • main-gtk has been dropped and will not work
  • main-x11 has been dropped and will not work

Replacing the above will be main-sdl. SDL (Simple Directmedia Layer) is a low-level gfx-toolkit (not gui-toolkit) which is highly portable (unix, win, mac, ...), and will provide sound-support directly for Langband. Code to handle images and fonts are also used with Langband. The default use of Langband in the future will be via the SDL-interface. The GUI is specified in a theme-file and you can easily change the look and feel of your langband-window through themes/skins.

Langband isn't limited to the old ASCII/Letter view. Only a click away (by mouse or keyboard) you can view the dungeon in great looking graphical tiles done by David Gervais:


In addition the birth-system has been improved and now looks much better.


The above changes are just some of many changes and updated to Langband v0.1.1.
Langband v0.1.1 is a developement release and will not be as stable or as easy to install as v0.1 though, but will be much prettier and have a few gameplay additions.
