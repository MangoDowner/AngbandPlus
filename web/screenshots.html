In v0.1.1 a lot of the user-interface was changed and Langband now uses SDL (Simple Directmedia Layer). SDL is a low-level gfx-toolkit (not gui-toolkit) which is highly portable (unix, win, mac, ...), and will provide sound-support directly for Langband. Code to handle images and fonts are also used with Langband. The default use of Langband in the future will be via the SDL-interface. Some screenhosts (in reduced size, click for full size):


The GUI is specified in a theme-file and you can easily change the look and feel of your langband-window through themes/skins.

Langband isn't limited to the old ASCII/Letter view. Only a click away (by mouse or keyboard) you can view the dungeon in great looking graphical tiles done by David Gervais:


In addition the birth-system has been improved and now looks much better.
