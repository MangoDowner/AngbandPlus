Experimental debian-packages

If you're a Debian-user (with either sid/unstable or woody) and want to test debian-packaging of Langband you now have the chance. Snapshots of Langband will frequently be created as packages at the following location:

  deb http://www.langband.org/debian local lisp
  deb-src http://www.langband.org/debian local lisp

The lines above may be added to /etc/apt/sources.list and you'll be notified whenever langband is updated. If you have no lisp-system working on your Debian, you might need the packages cmucl, cmucl-small and common-lisp-controller from Debian. Though CMUCL misses sound-effects. The cmucl in potato cannot be used for Langband because it does not provide callbacks for C-code.
