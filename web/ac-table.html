Armour-table vanilla

The armour-value describes a full set-up of armour, including helmet, shield, gloves, boots, cloak and body-armour. Parts of the outfit is expected to have appropriate enchantments, e.g a dragon-armour will always be slightly enchanted, just as a full-plate combat armour is enchanted to allow it's wearer to move at all. A creature with armour of 55 may have natural armour but it will be as tough as plated-armour and as such use the plated-armour description.
